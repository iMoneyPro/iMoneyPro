Auto-Renewable Subscriptions

  1. After TT Studio VIP membership is activated, it will automatically renew and extend the corresponding period 24 hours before the expiration of the current period;
  2. The automatic renewal service activated by the Apple account is a subscription relationship signed with Apple. You need to log in to the Apple account on the Apple mobile phone to cancel it. TT Studio has no right to cancel it. For the method, please refer to:
    -> open the "Settings" of iPhone 
-> enter "iTunes Store and App Store" 
-> click "Apple ID" 
-> enter "Account Settings" 
-> click "Subscribe"
-> select "TT Studio", unsubscribe
  3. If you need to cancel the subscription, please turn off the automatic renewal at least 24 hours before the deduction;
  4. Due to Apple ID restrictions, the actual price paid by iOS users is subject to the actual display of Apple Pay.
